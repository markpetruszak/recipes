ITC Style Dal Bukhara
Recipe for slow cooked Dal Bukhara, ITC style made with black urad dal -
black lentils. Measurements Used - 1 Cup = 250ml
Author: Kannamma - Suguna Vinodh
Recipe type: Gravy
Cuisine: Indian
Serves: 6
Ingredients
1 cup Urad Dal
2 tablespoon oil
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
2 sticks cinnamon
2 medium onions, chopped
1 pod garlic and 2 inch piece ginger, pureed to a paste with ¼ cup water
8 Tomatoes
2 teaspoon chilli powder
1 teaspoon turmeric powder
2 teaspoon cumin powder
1.5 teaspoon salt
½ cup cream
3 tablespoon unsalted butter
